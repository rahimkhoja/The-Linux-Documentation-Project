11.2 80386 (and more) Descriptors

Overview

Descriptors are data structure used by Intel microprocessor i386+ to virtualize memory.

Kind of descriptors

  • GDT (Global Descriptor Table)
  • LDT (Local Descriptor Table)
  • IDT (Interrupt Descriptor Table)
