Subscribe to LinuxFocus announcements

Readers who would like to be informed about new issues of LinuxFocus may subscribe to the announce Mailing list.
Your e-mail address will not be disclosed to anybody outside the LinuxFocus team and you can unsubscribe at any time.
If you use only a temporary e-mail address (e.g e-mail account at university) then please remember to unsubscribe before the address becomes invalid (e.g you have left university).
Question: How can I subscribe?
Answer: Send an E-Mail to majordomo@linuxfocus.org and write in the body of the mail (not the subject!!):
subscribe announce 
end
This mail has to be an plain ascii mail not a html mail.
Note: you can only subscribe yourself. We do not approve subscription attempts where the subscription address is different from the sender of the mail. You will also get a confirmation mail. In other words you can not subscribe somebody else.
Question: How do I unsubscribe?
Answer: Send an E-Mail to majordomo@linuxfocus.org and write in the body of the mail (not the subject!!):
unsubscribe announce your_email@used.at.subscription
end
