    G> What's wdiff?

It's one of those cool comp.unix.sources utilities no one should be
without yet no one bundles it; it is almost lost to obscurity.

wdiff is 'diff' but ignores whitespace.  It is ideal for creating
diffs of textfiles where an added CR or TABs swapped for space is
meaningless.  It was originally designed as a utility to work with
LaTeX files.
