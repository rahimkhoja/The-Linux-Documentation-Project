The Gnome Documentation Project has put the contents of the GPL and the
FDL online, in DocBook SGML format. The URL is
http://developer.gnome.org/projects/gdp/licenses.html

I would like to thank Eric Baudais, who actually did the markup, as well
as Dan Mueth and the rest of the GDP for sharing this with us.

Mark Komarinski, we should add a link to this page into our LDP Author
Guide, so authors who wish to use the GPL or the FDL know where to find
them to include in their documents.
