Hello all,

I just read that the GDP (Gnome Documentation Project) has put the GFDL
and the GPL into DocBook format, for easy inclusion in documents.

See
http://developer.gnome.org/projects/gdp/status/GDP_status_report_3.html

This article also has some interesting information about GUI DocBook
editors. Take a look at Conglomerate, if you haven't already. Also,
AbiWord is adding DocBook support. Things really seem to be happening
fast lately in our little world, don't they?
