Montreal, April 4, 2000 - Press Release - For immediate release

Jon Maddog Hall, Director of Linux International
will join the biggest international players in the Linux and OSS market
for the Keynote Session on Tuesday, April 11.
LINUX EXPO 2000 NORTH AMERICA
PALAIS DES CONGRÈS, MONTREAL, APRIL 10 - 11 & 12
A Canadian First

Jon Maddog Hall, Linux International's Director, will be attending the Keynote
Session to be held Tuesday, April 11 at 8:30 a.m. at Linux Expo North America.

A forerunner and one of the master builders behind the development of Linux, Jon
Maddog Hall is well known in information technology circles.  He has been an
integral part of the industry for the past 25 years as a software engineer,
systems administrator, product manager, marketing director and professor.  His
students dubbed him Maddog and the nickname stuck.

The Keynote Session will bring together the biggest international players in the
Linux market.  The speakers will include:
Bob Young, President, Red Hat,
Michael Cowpland, CEO, Corel Corporation,
Eric S. Raymond, President, Open Source Initiative,
Richard Stallman,
Larry Augustin, CEO, VA Linux,
Jacques le Marois, President, MandrakeSoft,
Dirk Hondel, CTO, SuSe,
Jeremy Burton, VP Server Technology, Oracle Corporation.

Linux Expo North America will take place from April 10 to 12 at Montreal's
Palais des Congrès. Visitors can register at www.linuxexpocanada.com.

Linux Expo is organized by the Sky Events Group, organizer of the Linux Expo
shows in Paris (June 1999 and February 2000) and around the world (Madrid,
Toronto, Sao Paulo, Shanghai).
