speaking of keywords and descriptions and the like. if you were to simply
enter each HOW-TO (or each author could do this) into the OMF template
which includes fields for KEYWORDS and DESCRIPTION. this would give you
XML-coded, Dublin Core compliant metadata records that could then be
searched by our search engine and/or we'll be glad to share the records
with others. this would help us get a greater collection of sharable
standards-based metadata and help folks find the HOW-TO they really want.
http://metalab.unc.edu/osrt/omf/ will get you to the general OMF
information page.
http://metalab.unc.edu/osrt/omf/cgi-bin/template.pl will take you
directly to the template.
