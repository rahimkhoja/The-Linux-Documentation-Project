Would not a searchable index based on keywords not be a good idea too?
Just speaking as a regular user, I would love to have something
similar to 'apropos'. Something quick and easy to pop up a list of
relevant docs.

I don't think technically this would be very challenging at all. In
fact, with just some fairly straightforward scripting I think
something meagerly passable could be done, and would be quite useful.

The real work would be getting the keywords in there to start with.
And then LinuxDoc vs DocBook is a fly in the ointment too.

And yes, if it seems workable, I would volunteer to the extent of what
I know. <g>
