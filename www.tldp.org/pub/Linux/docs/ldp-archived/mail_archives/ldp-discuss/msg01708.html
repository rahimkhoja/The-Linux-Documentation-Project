Notice to journalists, editors, reporters

The registration procedure for Linux Expo 2000 North America is now on line. The
journalists, who wish to be accredited to cover the event, to attend the
conferences and to use the logistics of the press room, are invited to register
through the website : http://www.skyevents.com/EN/mediasen.php3

Linux Expo 2000 North America devoted to OSS software and Linux solutions will
take place at Montreal's Palais des Congrès from April 10 to 12.
