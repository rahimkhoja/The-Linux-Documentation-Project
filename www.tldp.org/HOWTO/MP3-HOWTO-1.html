1. Introduction.

This document describes the hardware, software and procedures needed to encode, play, mix and stream MP3 sound files under Linux.

It covers:

  • Encoding MP3's from a live or external source
  • Encoding MP3's from audio CD's
  • Streaming MP3's over a network
  • Listening to MP3's
  • Recording MP3 streams
  • Mixing MP3's
  • Editing ID3 information
