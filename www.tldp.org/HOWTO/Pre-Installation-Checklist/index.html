This document provides a checklist for installing GNU/Linux (hereafter referred to as Linux) as well as hints and tips along the way. Translations in Japanese, Dutch, and French are available in Section 1.3. The most current version of this checklist will be posted online at: http://members.tripod.com/~algolog/lnxchk.htm. Sgml source for this document is located here.
