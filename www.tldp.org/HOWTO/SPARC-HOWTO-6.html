6. Sun's Netra servers

GNU/Linux is known to run on the following systems:

  • SBUS based Netra
    • Netra i
  • PCI based Netra
    • Netra T
    • Netra X1
Installation kernel images can be downloaded from 
http://sunsite.ulatina.ac.cr/Unix/Linux/Splack/
sparc/splack-8.0/kernels/sun4u.s
