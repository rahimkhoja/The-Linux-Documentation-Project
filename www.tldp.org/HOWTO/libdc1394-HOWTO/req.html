2. Requirements

Various libraries and modules required are:

  • Library: libdc1394-1.1.0

  • Supporting Library: libraw1394-1.2.0

  • Supporting Modules: ieee1394, raw1394, ohci1394, video1394

  • Linux Kernel: 2.6.10 or above
