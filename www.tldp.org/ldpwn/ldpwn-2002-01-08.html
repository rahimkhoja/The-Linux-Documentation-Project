LDP Weekly News
2002-01-08

by David Merrill
 

Relicensing Continues

We continue to receive document updates that include a relicensing in response to Debian packaging issues. Much of the LDP will likely be removed from the base Debian distribution if they are not licensed under a Free license. Almost all the new documents are using the GFDL, so hopefully licensing issues will be behind us soon.

Get the LDPWN by Mail

For all of you who read the Linux Weekly News Updates about the LDP, you can now get them via email. Just send an email to news-subscribe@linuxdoc.org. The list is low traffic.

About the LDP

The Linux Documentation Project is developing free, high quality documentation for the GNU/Linux operating system. This includes the creation of HOWTOs and Guides, and collaboration with other documentation groups.

If you've always wanted to help Linux reach Total World Domination(tm), but you're not a programmer, there's still something you can do. Help the LDP!

The LDP keeps a page of resources for authors at http://www.linuxdoc.org/authors/. Contributions are always welcome.

For more LDP Weekly News, go to http://www.linuxdoc.org/ldpwn/
