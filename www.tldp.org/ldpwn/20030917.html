1. New documents

1.1. Proposed new documents

  • Martin A. Brown, the author of Traffic Control tcng-HTB HOWTO, wrote a new one plainly called Traffic Control HOWTO. It is more detailed than the first HOWTO, introducing core concepts of traffic control systems to users and system administrators who want to deploy traffic control in their networks. Martin is eager to publish it and awaits your comments.
  • Expect a Installing and Running Linux under a Virtual PC from Chris Gehlker.

1.2. Submitted for review

  • Christophe Devine posted the first draft of his Encrypted Root Filesystem HOWTO. The document, like any other that gets into the collection needs review. Over to Tabatha.

2. Updates

2.1. HOWTOs

  • Call us security freaks or paranoid, our data is a matter of privacy. If you agree with this, check out v1.1 of David Braun's Disk Encryption HOWTO.
  • Daniel Quintiliani has extended his Xnews under Linux HOWTO. Since he is covering more newsreaders in v2.0, he has renamed his HOWTO as the Windows Newsreaders in Linux HOWTO.
  • Svetoslav Slavtchev has updated his XFree Local Multi-User HOWTO to v1.2.

2.2. Guides

  • Mendel Cooper has been maintaining the Advanced Bash-Scripting Guide for quite some time now. After over 3 years, it is now at v2.1.
  • Find out everything about your filesystem in Binh Nguyen's Linux Filesystem Hierarchy documentation, v0.61.

3. Feedback and Contributions

You can help The Linux Documentation Project by:

  • Reviewing one of the draft versions of the documents in progress.
  • Picking up one of the unmaintained documents.
  • Providing assistance with markup.

Please help us create this weekly newsletter. Seen a site putting in a story with a link to the LDP? Completed the translation of any HOWTO? Know of any articles about the LDP published in newspapers or magazines? You can update us by sending in links and information at <feedback@en.tldp.org>.
