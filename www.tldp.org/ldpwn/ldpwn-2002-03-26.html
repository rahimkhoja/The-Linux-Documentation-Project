LDP Weekly News
2002-03-26

by David Merrill
 

"Introduction to..." Documents

This week we've created a new document category at the LDP, beginning with five documents from Jenn Vesperman of LinuxChix. Jenn's documents are introductions to basic computing subjects, and unlike most LDP documents they are not unique to Linux. In fact, they do not cover the details of any operating system or application, but instead they focus on the underlying concepts. They should be very helpful for people who are newbies not only to Linux but to computing itself. We hope to publish more documents like these in the future.

These documents are listed at http://www.linuxdoc.org/docs.html#intro

About the LDP

The Linux Documentation Project is developing free, high quality documentation for the GNU/Linux operating system. This includes the creation of HOWTOs and Guides, and collaboration with other documentation groups.

If you've always wanted to help Linux reach Total World Domination(tm), but you're not a programmer, there's still something you can do. Help the LDP!

The LDP keeps a page of resources for authors at http://www.linuxdoc.org/authors/. Contributions are always welcome.

For more LDP Weekly News, go to http://www.linuxdoc.org/ldpwn/
