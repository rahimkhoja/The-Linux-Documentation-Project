Chief Coordinator

The chief coordinator provides a consistent direction for TLDP's efforts and promotes TLDP's public image.

Responsibilities

  • Participate in TLDP's general discussion mailing list.
  • Stay apprised of current events at TLDP.

Commitment

Long-term commitment is required. Consistent daily effort is required to keep up with discussions on the mailing lists and current events at TLDP.

Qualifications

General knowledge of how free software and free documentation work. Good organizational skills.

Supervision / Direct Reports

Peer feedback is obtained from TLDP's general discussion mailing list. The chief coordinator oversees the various coordinator positions within TLDP.

Training

Knowledge transfer from previous person filling this role.

Policies Affecting Assignment

The chief coordinator represents TLDP and should therefore be able to separate his/her personal opinion from the official TLDP position.

Benefits Of The Position

Experience in project management. Publicity.
