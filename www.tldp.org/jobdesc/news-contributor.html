News Contributor

News contributors promote TLDP by communicating events and accomplishments to the public.

Responsibilities

  • Write short articles or press releases related to TLDP.
  • Fact-check all submissions.
  • Submit news as an attachment or hyperlink via email.
  • Work with news editors to correct any errors or omissions.

Commitment

Generally, two or three hour per month is required to be a regular news contributor. One-time submissions and press releases are also encouraged.

Qualifications

A familiarity with current events at TLDP is required as are good written language skills. Experience writing press releases is a plus.

Supervision

The final decision regarding publication rests with the TLDP news editors.

Training

There is no formal training for this position.

Policies Affecting Assignment

Volunteers should contact the news editors and announce their intention to contribute.

Benefits Of The Position

A news contributor gain writing experience while at the same time promoting their favorite TLDP projects.
