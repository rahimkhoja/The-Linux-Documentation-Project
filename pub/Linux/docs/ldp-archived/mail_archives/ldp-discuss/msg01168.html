On Wed, Jan 26, 2000 at 01:55:30PM -0500, Gary Lawrence Murphy wrote:
> What do you think of the idea of assigning a unique email address to
> each doc so the maintainer email can be easily changed without
> changing the doc itself?

I think it's simpler for each author to put his email in his HOWTO.
This allows direct contact.  As soon as a new maintainer takes over, a
new version needs to be immediately created so that the email address
at LDP will be correct.  Also, if someone new takes over maintaining a
doc, any mail sent to the old maintainer should be forwarded to the
new one.  While the above proposal has some merit I think we have more
important tasks to concentrate on.
