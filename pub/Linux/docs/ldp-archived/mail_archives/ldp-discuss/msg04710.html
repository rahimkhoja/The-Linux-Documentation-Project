Houston InterWeb Design, Inc.
 It's Role in the E-Marketplace

Houston InterWeb Design is a fully reporting company trading on the Over the Counter Bulletin Board under the ticker symbol (OTCBB: HITD - news). Houston InterWeb focuses primarily on assisting start-up Internet companies that need management knowledge and technological expertise. The Company's holdings include 100% ownership of Political Team, 20% interest in OTC News Network Inc., 20% interest in AMP3.com, 100% interest in Cyberpitch.com, Inc., 33% interest in Beijing Artists Online L.L.C., and 30% interest in Brazil InterWeb Design. The Company assists its customers in improving their Internet presence for products or services offered. The Company uses proprietary technology for the creation of Websites, which increases the chances that the Company's customers' Websites are seen by an Internet user irrespective of the search engine used.
