The following position has been proposed by several people on the list.

Public Relations Specialist (proposed)

The Public Relations specialist maintains the image of TLDP.

Responsibilities

A public relations specialist acts a point of contact between TLDP and the outside world. This position is responsible for working with representatives from media, Linux vendors, Linux user groups, and other parties interested in TLDP.

Training

All new public relations specialists are encouraged to read the Guide to Managing Media and Public Relations in the Linux Community .

Policies Affecting Assignment

All public relations specialists must have the approval of TLDP.

Benefits Of The Position

Being a public relations specialist gives volunteers the opportunity to interact with members of the Linux community.
