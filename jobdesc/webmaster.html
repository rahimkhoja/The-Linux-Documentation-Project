Webmaster

The webmaster maintains the quality of TLDP's website.

Responsibilities

  • Publish updates to ancillary (non-published document) pages.
  • Maintain collection of links (links pages/database).
  • Act as a liaison between TLDP and Ibiblio for issues related to the website.

Commitment

The time commitment is less than 1 hour per week.

Qualifications

The webmaster should have a good understanding of HTML, CSS and BASH scripting. Some knowledge of JavaScript is beneficial.

Supervision

Updates are usually done in response to items discussed on the general TLDP discussion list, or from the feedback list.

Training

There is no formal training for this position.

Policies Affecting Assignment

The webmaster needs to establish a user account and password with Ibiblio and this account is subject to Ibiblio's network usage policy.

Benefits Of The Position

The webmaster can gain valuable experience managing a website that receives over two-hundred fifty thousand hits per day.
