New Documents and Documents In Progress

If you are working on a new HOWTO, FAQ, or Guide; or have an idea/topic for a new document please send your proposal (and a brief introduction about yourself) to the LDP's discussion list (discuss@en.tldp.org). Note: you must be subscribed to post. Subscription information is available from: http://tldp.org/mailinfo.html#maillists
